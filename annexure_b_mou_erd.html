Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) — MOU Tracking System

Logical data model for core entities used in Direct MOU and engagement workflows: Users, Proposals, Sector Leads, MOU Documents, Activities, Complaints, Engagement Rooms, and Party B profiles. Cardinality notation: 1 / N.

Cardinality & relationship notes

Users 1 — N Proposals — Party A owns / submits many MOUs
Users 1 — 0..1 Party B — optional Party B profile row
Users 1 — N Sector Leads — sector assignment scopes
Proposals 1 — N MOU Documents — file versions over time
Proposals 1 — N Activities — Progress / update requests
Proposals 1 — 0..N Complaints — optional dispute records
Proposals 1 — 1 Engagement Room — chat scoped to approved MOU
Party B 1 — N Proposals — one account may link to many MOUs
Solid lines — primary ownership / FK
Dashed lines — actor participation (filed_by, added_by, sender)
