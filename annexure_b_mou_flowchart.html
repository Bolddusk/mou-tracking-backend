System Flow Chart — MOU Tracking System

End-to-end Direct MOU lifecycle: Party A submission, Sector Lead review, approval with Party B provisioning, MOU upload, dual acknowledgment, and deal close.

Flow summary (happy path)

  1. Submit — Party A completes Direct MOU wizard and submits.
  2. Review — Sector Lead (or Super Admin) reviews the proposal.
  3. Decision — Reject → revise/resubmit; Approve → continue.
  4. Provision — System creates or links Party B (and Party A contact) accounts.
  5. Upload — Signed/working MOU file is uploaded (version history kept).
  6. Acknowledge — Party A and Party B acknowledge the MOU.
  7. Close — Authorized staff closes the deal; lifecycle completes.
